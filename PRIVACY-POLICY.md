# Privacy Policy - CountOnMe Discord Bot

**Effective Date:** August 6, 2025  
**Last Updated:** August 6, 2025

## 1. Introduction

CountOnMe ("we," "our," or "the bot") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our Discord bot service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use CountOnMe bot, we automatically collect:

**Discord Identifiers:**
- Discord server (guild) IDs
- Discord user IDs
- Discord channel IDs where the bot is used
- Discord message IDs for counting verification

**Game Statistics:**
- Correct counting attempts
- Incorrect counting attempts  
- Highest numbers reached
- Weekly performance statistics
- Trophy and achievement data
- Time spent with temporary roles

**Technical Data:**
- Timestamps of bot interactions
- Command usage patterns
- Error logs for debugging purposes

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:
- Real names or personal identifiers
- Email addresses or contact information
- Message content (except for counting numbers)
- Voice data or multimedia content
- Location or IP address information
- Information from other Discord servers where the bot is not present

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

Your information is used exclusively to:
- Provide counting game functionality
- Generate leaderboards and statistics
- Manage weekly competitions and trophy systems
- Enforce game rules through temporary role assignments
- Improve bot performance and fix bugs

### 3.2 Data Processing Legal Basis

We process your data based on:
- **Legitimate Interest:** Providing the requested bot functionality
- **Consent:** By adding the bot to your server, you consent to data processing
- **Performance of Service:** Data processing necessary to deliver bot features

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Data is stored securely on Railway cloud infrastructure
- Database files use industry-standard SQLite with WAL journaling
- Automatic backups are created to prevent data loss
- Data is stored in persistent volumes to survive service restarts

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Control:** Strict access controls limit who can view data
- **Monitoring:** Automated monitoring for unusual access patterns
- **Regular Updates:** Security patches applied promptly

### 4.3 Data Retention

**Active Data:**
- Game statistics: Retained indefinitely for leaderboard functionality
- Server configuration: Retained while bot remains in server
- User preferences: Retained for consistent user experience

**Temporary Data:**
- Temporary role assignments: Automatically deleted when expired
- Debug logs: Retained for 30 days maximum
- Backup files: Older backups deleted automatically (5 most recent kept)

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

### 5.1 No Data Sales
- We never sell your personal information to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising purposes
- We do not share data with data brokers or marketing companies

### 5.2 Service Providers
We may share data with trusted service providers only when necessary:
- **Railway:** Cloud hosting provider (infrastructure only)
- **Discord:** Platform where the bot operates (API communications only)

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by:
- Valid legal process or court order
- Discord Terms of Service enforcement
- Protection of our rights and safety of users

## 6. Your Rights and Controls

### 6.1 Data Access
You can request to see what data we have collected about your Discord user ID by contacting us through our GitHub repository.

### 6.2 Data Deletion
- **Individual Users:** Request deletion of your personal statistics
- **Server Administrators:** Remove the bot to stop data collection
- **Complete Removal:** Contact us for complete data deletion

### 6.3 Data Portability
Upon request, we can provide your data in a machine-readable format.

### 6.4 Corrections
If you believe any collected data is inaccurate, you may request corrections through our support channels.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

CountOnMe bot does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies. All data collection is limited to what is necessary for bot functionality within Discord.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our bot does not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years of age. If we become aware that a user under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete such information immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in countries other than where you are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers.

## 10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects personal information:
- We will investigate and contain the breach immediately
- Affected users will be notified within 72 hours when possible
- We will cooperate with authorities as required by law

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

### 11.1 Notification of Changes
- We will announce significant privacy policy changes
- Notifications will be made through GitHub releases and bot announcements
- Continued use after changes indicates acceptance

### 11.2 Review Frequency
This privacy policy is reviewed annually or when significant changes to data processing occur.

This privacy policy complies with:
